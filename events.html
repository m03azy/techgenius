Events & Activities

TechGenius is active in the community through regular events, programs, and initiatives that build skills and inspire the next generation.

Programs & Activities

Upcoming & Recurring Events

📅

Monthly Digital Skills Workshops

Open to the public, held every 2nd Saturday of the month. These hands-on sessions cover essential digital tools and practical skills for individuals and professionals. Venue announced monthly — follow @techgenius_tz for updates.

🚀

Quarterly Hackathons

Innovation challenges for youth and students to build real digital solutions within a time-bound, team-based format. Winners gain mentorship, recognition, and support to develop their ideas further.

🎤

Annual Tech Summit

A full-day conference on technology, entrepreneurship, and digital transformation in Tanzania. Features keynote speakers, panel discussions, exhibitions, and networking opportunities for tech professionals, students, and business leaders.

👨‍🏫

Career Day Events

Partnership events with schools and universities for career guidance and mentorship sessions. Students get direct access to industry professionals, internship opportunities, and practical career planning advice.

💻

School Outreach Programs

Free digital literacy sessions for secondary school students across Arusha. TechGenius volunteers visit schools to deliver engaging, age-appropriate lessons on digital tools, online safety, and career pathways in tech.

🤝

Partner Engagement Forums

Quarterly stakeholder meetings with partners, donors, and community leaders. These forums review progress, explore collaboration opportunities, and align on shared goals for digital empowerment across Tanzania.
